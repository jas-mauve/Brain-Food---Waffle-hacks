Brain Food - Fuel for school!




Brain Food is a website for students with food insecurity. Our tagline "Fuel For School" is specifically about providing solutions or resources to problems related to daily meals and cost. Here at Brain Food, students can find affordable healthy recipes, a locator to food banks near them and important information. Brain Food also has a Sale Radar where people can post or view grocery store deals in their area.
