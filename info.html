Food insecurity

Food insecurity is defined as the disruption of food intake or eating patterns because of lack of money and other resources. The first steps of solving food insicurity is understanding it. Below you will find the overwhelming statistics of how deep this crisis really is.

55% of first generation students identify as food insecure


2.05$ is how much money a food insecure person has to live off of per day.


39% of college students face food insecurity


45% of deaths among children are caused by hunger


3 million children die of hunger globally
